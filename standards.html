OUR STANDARDS

We know that your home is your sanctuary, so we promise to treat it exactly as if it were our own. From the very first conversation to the final finishing touches, we’re here to make sure your project is handled with the care, respect, and attention to detail it deserves.

For us, "treating your home like our own" means more than just being tidy—it means holding ourselves to the highest possible standards. We’ve gone the extra mile to earn the industry's most respected accreditations so you can sit back and relax, knowing you’re in safe, professional hands. Here is exactly what our "badges of honor" mean for you:

20-YEAR GUARANTEE

As soon as the tools are all packed away, and we’ve finished transforming your home, we’ll get your official guarantee dated and sent over to you! We want you to feel totally relaxed about your new investment, which is why our guarantee covers you for a full 20 years. Why so long? Simply because we’re confident in the quality of what we build. We use materials that are made to go the distance, so you don't have to worry about a thing.

To make sure you get the most out of your 20-year guarantee, it’s worth noting a couple of exceptions. Generally, things like accidents or extreme weather damage aren't covered. It’s also really important that we’re the only ones who work on the installation—having someone else (or yourself) modify or repair it would mean we can no longer guarantee the work. All the details are tucked away in your project paperwork if you need them!

CHAS

Being CHAS Accredited is our way of proving that safety is our top priority. It means we’ve been independently vetted to ensure our health and safety standards are second to none, giving you the confidence that your home project will be managed responsibly, safely, and to the highest professional level.

GGF

As a member of the Glass and Glazing Federation (GGF), we follow the industry’s strictest code of conduct. This means you’re protected by high technical standards, clear consumer contracts, and a free conciliation service—giving you an extra layer of protection and confidence that your installation is in expert hands.

Constructionline Gold

Our Constructionline Gold status is a "Gold Standard" badge of approval, proving we’ve been independently vetted for top-tier safety, quality, and reliability. It’s your shortcut to total peace of mind, knowing your project is in the most professional hands.

FENSA

Being FENSA accreditation is your guarantee that every window and door we install meets strict building and energy regulations. It’s your shortcut to a high-quality job, providing you with the essential certificate you’ll need if you ever sell your home, while saving you the hassle of extra council inspections.

FCA

Our FCA accreditation means we’re officially authorized to offer flexible finance options for your project. It’s your guarantee that we follow strict rules to treat you fairly, provide clear and honest financial information, and help you find a payment plan that truly fits your budget.
